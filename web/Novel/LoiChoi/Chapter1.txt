The singer revealed that the album "Loi Choi" derives its name from the way his father used to call him when he was a child. He confessed that although his father isn't involved in the music industry, he has always been supportive and played a significant role in his career. Wren Evans believes that the album is a gift that he wants to dedicate to his father. Following the debut song "Thích Em Hơi Nhiều," the "Loi Choi" album once again features retro music reminiscent of the 2000s, with transitions and sound materials that transport us back to 2023. Additionally, the album incorporates strong Flamenco music, evoking tropical vibes, and the trending Jersey Club genre in the international underground scene.
